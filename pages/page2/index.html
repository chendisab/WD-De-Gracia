Skills

Kagura, a mage hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, wields the mystical Seimei Umbrella with mastery over Yin and Yang magic. Her passive, Yin Yang Gathering, grants her a shield and stuns nearby enemies when she retrieves her umbrella. With her first skill, Seimei Umbrella Open, she throws her umbrella to deal magic damage in a designated area.

Skills

Using Rasho Umbrella Flee as her second skill, Kagura removes debuffs and dashes in a chosen direction, leaving her umbrella behind. Her ultimate, Yin Yang Overturn, has two forms: with the umbrella, she pulls enemies towards it, dealing damage and slowing them; without it, she releases a damaging wave of energy that knocks back foes, with the returning umbrella adding additional damage.

Story

Kagura, from the prestigious Onmyouji family, wields the ancient Seimei Umbrella. A prodigy in Yin and Yang magic, she ventures into the Land of Dawn to hone her skills and protect her loved ones, becoming a formidable force on the battlefield.
