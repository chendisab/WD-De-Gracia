Lunox

Lunox, the Twilight Goddess in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, commands both light and dark magic, embodying balance and chaos. Her passive, Dreamland Twist, allows her to switch between Order and Chaos states, each enhancing different abilities. With her first skill, Starlight Pulse (Order)/Chaos Assault (Chaos), Lunox releases energy that damages and penetrates enemies. Her second skill, Power of Order (Order)/Chaos Blink (Chaos), grants her mobility and enhances her next basic attack. Lunox's ultimate, Cosmic Fission, unleashes a burst of energy that deals damage in a wide area, scaling with her magic power. Born with the gift of dual magic, Lunox navigates a world where light and darkness vie for dominance. She seeks to maintain equilibrium, using her powers to thwart chaos and protect the realms from imbalance. Lunox's journey is a constant struggle to control her dual nature while striving to bring harmony to the Land of Dawn, where her presence is both feared and revered.

Skills

Kagura, a mage hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, wields the mystical Seimei Umbrella with mastery over Yin and Yang magic. Her passive, Yin Yang Gathering, grants her a shield and stuns nearby enemies when she retrieves her umbrella. With her first skill, Seimei Umbrella Open, she throws her umbrella to deal magic damage in a designated area.

Skills

Using Rasho Umbrella Flee as her second skill, Kagura removes debuffs and dashes in a chosen direction, leaving her umbrella behind. Her ultimate, Yin Yang Overturn, has two forms: with the umbrella, she pulls enemies towards it, dealing damage and slowing them; without it, she releases a damaging wave of energy that knocks back foes, with the returning umbrella adding additional damage.

Story

Kagura, from the prestigious Onmyouji family, wields the ancient Seimei Umbrella. A prodigy in Yin and Yang magic, she ventures into the Land of Dawn to hone her skills and protect her loved ones, becoming a formidable force on the battlefield.

Odette

MLBB Mage

Odette, the Swan Princess of Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, draws power from her lineage and the tranquil Swan Lake where she was raised. Her passive, Lakeshore Ambience, enhances her magic power after skill use. Avian Authority sends her swan forward to damage and slow enemies, while Blue Nova summons a swirling energy that damages and immobilizes foes. Her ultimate, Swan Song, creates a damaging symphony that slows enemies before culminating in a powerful explosion. Odette's journey began when dark forces threatened her homeland, prompting her to harness her elegant water-based magic to defend against the Abyssal Lord. She has since become a beacon of courage, forging alliances with other heroes to ensure peace prevails in the Land of Dawn.

VEXANA

Vexana, the Necromancer in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, wields dark magic to command the undead and bring terror to her foes. Her passive, Necromancy Spell, marks enemies, causing them to explode upon death and deal damage to nearby targets.

Skills

Vexana’s ultimate, Cursed Oath, summons a puppet from a marked enemy, mimicking their abilities to fight for her.

Story

Once a noble queen, Vexana's life was shattered when she succumbed to the allure of dark magic to save her dying kingdom. The corruption turned her into a necromancer, and her realm fell into ruin. Now, she roams the Land of Dawn, driven by vengeance and a desire to reclaim her lost glory

Pharsa

Pharsa, the Wings of Vengeance in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, commands mystical energy and flight. Her passive, Spiritual Unity, turns her into mist after using skills, boosting speed and granting immunity to crowd control. She marks and slows enemies with Curse of Crow, releases a damaging blast with Energy Impact, and rains devastating aerial attacks with Feathered Air Strike. Once a princess blinded by dark forces, Pharsa made a pact with the Crow Spirit, Verri, to restore her vision. Now, seeking vengeance against those who wronged her people, she uses her formidable magic and aerial prowess to protect the Land of Dawn.
