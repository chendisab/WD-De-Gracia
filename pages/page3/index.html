Odette

MLBB Mage

Odette, the Swan Princess of Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, draws power from her lineage and the tranquil Swan Lake where she was raised. Her passive, Lakeshore Ambience, enhances her magic power after skill use. Avian Authority sends her swan forward to damage and slow enemies, while Blue Nova summons a swirling energy that damages and immobilizes foes. Her ultimate, Swan Song, creates a damaging symphony that slows enemies before culminating in a powerful explosion. Odette's journey began when dark forces threatened her homeland, prompting her to harness her elegant water-based magic to defend against the Abyssal Lord. She has since become a beacon of courage, forging alliances with other heroes to ensure peace prevails in the Land of Dawn.
